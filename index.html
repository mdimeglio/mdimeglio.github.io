About

I am a PhD student in the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh.

I am interested in applied category theory, especially applications of category theory to computer science. My research so far has focused on delta lenses—a category theoretic model of bidirectional transformations, polynomial functors and indexed categories.
